Motion Blur Steps

1. Select your clip in CapCut.

2. Go to Effects → Video Effects → Motion Blur.

3. Adjust intensity to 50-70.

Color Grading Steps

1. Select Adjust option.

2. Increase Contrast +10.

3. Reduce Saturation -10.

4. Increase Temperature +5.

Black Bars Steps

1. Go to Canvas.

2. Select Format 2.35:1.

Slow Motion Steps

1. Select clip.

2. Click Speed → Curve → Smooth Slow Motion.
