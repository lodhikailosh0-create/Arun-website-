CapCut me Cinematic Video Edit Kaise Kare (Step by Step Hindi Guide)

Agar aap Instagram Reels ya YouTube Shorts ke liye cinematic video banana chahte ho, to CapCut sabse best free app hai. Is guide me hum step by step sikhenge cinematic edit ka full process.

Step 1 – Video Import kare

CapCut open kare → New Project → Apna video select kare.

Step 2 – Cinematic Ratio set kare

Format me jaake 16:9 ya 9:16 select kare (Reels ke liye 9:16).

Step 3 – Color Grading (Cinematic look)

Adjust option me jaakar ye settings kare:

  • Brightness: -10
  • Contrast: +20
  • Saturation: -10
  • Sharpen: +30
  • Temperature: +10

Step 4 – Motion Blur Effect lagaye

Effects → Video Effects → Motion Blur → Apply to all clips.

Step 5 – Cinematic Black Bars lagaye

Overlay → Add overlay → Black bar PNG add kare top & bottom.

Step 6 – Smooth Slow Motion

Clip select kare → Speed → Curve → Montage select kare.

Step 7 – Cinematic Music add kare

Audio → Sounds → Soft cinematic music add kare.

Step 8 – Export Best Quality me

Export settings:

  • Resolution: 1080p
  • FPS: 60
  • Code rate: High

Conclusion

In steps ko follow karke aap CapCut me professional cinematic video bana sakte ho. Practice karte raho aur apni reels ko next level pe le jao.
