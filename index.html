CapCut me Cinematic Video Edit Kaise Kare (Step by Step)

Agar aap Instagram Reels ya YouTube Shorts ke liye cinematic video banana chahte ho, to CapCut sabse best free app hai. Is guide me hum step by step sikhenge cinematic edit ka full process.

Step 1 – Video Import kare

CapCut open kare → New Project → Apna video select kare.

Step 2 – Cinematic Ratio set kare

Format me jaake 9:16 select kare (Reels ke liye best).

Step 3 – Color Grading (Cinematic look)

  • Brightness: -10
  • Contrast: +20
  • Saturation: -10
  • Sharpen: +30
  • Temperature: +10

Step 4 – Motion Blur Effect

Effects → Video Effects → Motion Blur → Apply to all clips.

Step 5 – Black Bars (Cinematic)

Overlay me black bars PNG add kare top & bottom.

Step 6 – Smooth Slow Motion

Speed → Curve → Montage select kare.

Step 7 – Cinematic Music

Soft cinematic music add kare for professional feel.

Step 8 – Export Best Quality

  • Resolution: 1080p
  • FPS: 60
  • Code rate: High
